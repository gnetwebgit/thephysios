Run Lab

Run Lab is a tailored assessment and treatment package specifically designed for runners of all levels

Run lab is tailored assessment and treatment package specifically designed for runners fo all levels. Run Lab aims to prevent running injuries, treat persistence problems, and/or improve your running performance.

Common injuries to benefit from Run Lab include:

  • Plantar Fasciitis
  • Achilles Tendinopathy
  • shin spilnts, Runners Knee
  • SITB Friction syndrome
  • General persistence Niggles or injuries

consisting of bespoke sessions physio Jon Grayson will assess your running technique using slow motion video analysis and functional screen your mobility, stability, strength and agility. He will then design specific strength and conditioning programme improve your weekness and keep you running injury free in the long term. Check out your Runners Hub section to find out more.
